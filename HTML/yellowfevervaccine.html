Yellow Fever Vaccine — Key Information

Overview

The yellow fever vaccine is a live‑attenuated vaccine that typically provides long‑lasting immunity after a single dose and is used for prevention in endemic regions and for travelers to affected countries.

Use & Safety

Vaccination is required for entry to some countries; contraindications include severe immunosuppression and certain egg allergies. Rare serious adverse events are possible; follow national guidance.
