HIV Vaccine Research — Key Information

Overview

No licensed preventive HIV vaccine exists yet; research continues across multiple platforms including mRNA, viral vectors, and protein subunits.

Research Approaches

Strategies include eliciting broadly neutralizing antibodies, T‑cell vaccines, and prime‑boost regimens. Advances in technology are accelerating candidate development.

Current Prevention

While vaccines are in development, prevention relies on ART, PrEP, harm reduction, and testing/treatment programs.
