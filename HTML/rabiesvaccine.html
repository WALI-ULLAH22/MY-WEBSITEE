Rabies Vaccine — Key Information

Overview

Rabies vaccines are highly effective when used as pre‑exposure prophylaxis for at‑risk groups or as part of post‑exposure prophylaxis together with wound care and immunoglobulin when indicated.

Schedule & Use

Post‑exposure protocols include immediate wound cleaning, and a defined vaccine schedule; pre‑exposure vaccination is recommended for people at ongoing risk.

Control Strategies

Mass dog vaccination remains central to preventing dog‑mediated human rabies; access to vaccines and timely PEP is critical.
