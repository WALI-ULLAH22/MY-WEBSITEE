Zika Vaccine Research — Key Information

Overview

No licensed Zika vaccine is widely available yet; several candidates are in clinical trials, with priority to protect pregnant people and prevent congenital infection.

Research & Challenges

Challenges include demonstrating safety in pregnancy and achieving durable protection; platforms include inactivated, DNA/RNA, and viral vectors.
