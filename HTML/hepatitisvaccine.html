Hepatitis Vaccines — Key Information

Overview

Hepatitis A and B vaccines are effective tools to prevent acute and chronic liver disease. Hepatitis B vaccination at birth prevents chronic infections that lead to cirrhosis and liver cancer.

Schedule & Use

Hepatitis B is recommended as a multi-dose series starting at birth in many countries; Hepatitis A is given as two doses months apart where indicated.

Safety

Both vaccines have strong safety records; adverse events are generally mild. No vaccine currently exists for Hepatitis C.
