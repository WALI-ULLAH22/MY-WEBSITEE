Ebola Vaccine — Key Information

Overview

Ebola vaccines are used to prevent Ebola virus disease and to control outbreaks through ring vaccination and targeted campaigns.

Use & Effectiveness

Evidence from outbreak responses shows high effectiveness for licensed Ebola vaccines when used in ring vaccination strategies; they are deployed alongside surveillance and therapeutic care.

Safety

Safety monitoring occurs during deployment; most recipients experience mild, short‑lived side effects. Follow national guidance for contraindications and precautions.
